May 2026 · Specs & decision records

Castellan — delegated authority for local AI agents

Six specifications and thirteen architectural decision records for a proof-of-concept on delegated authority in local AI agent systems, applied to industrial maintenance work-order triage. Capability-broker architecture, hash-chained audit substrate, refusal as a first-class outcome. The thesis: an industrial buyer should not have to trust the AI — they should have to trust the authority broker and the audit substrate, which are small, inspectable, and behave like things they already know how to evaluate.
